Stefano Fantuzzi was born in 1980 in Italy. He grew up in a small village in the province of Treviso and he began to show interest in photography around the age of twenty. He took a short course in photography to learn the basic techniques, but then he decided to participate in various European volunteering projects for about two years, which led him to travel in Europe and learn French, English, and Spanish. After the volunteering experience he came to a conclusion that he needed more knowledge in photography and he finally enrol in a photography school in Padua where he studied for three years and started to look for his unique style. He then met the photographer Francesco Galifi and he became is assistant. In the meantime, he had participated in few small exhibitions with other photographers and in couple photography contests. After had lived abroad Stefano was feeling the need to move outside his country and now he is settle in London.
